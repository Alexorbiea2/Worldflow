To use the shader

1 Download **worldflow.blend**

2 In Blender go to **File -> Append**

3 Select **worldflow.blend**

4 Open the **NodeTree** folder

5 Pick **Worldflow** and click **Append**

6 Go to the **Shader Editor**

7 Switch the *Shader Editor* to **World**

8 In the *Shader Editor* do **Add -> Group -> worldflow**

9 **Delete** the default **Background** node and **World Output**

The shader will now be active.

On the **node group** right click then **Ungroup** or press **Ctrl + Alt + G**
Most explanations and notes are inside the node group itself

Designed for Eevee but probobly works in Cycles too Made in blender version 5.0.1
